    What this model doesn't capture

    Honest limitations of the simulation

    Contact networks Viral load variation Age-based susceptibility Indoor ventilation Vaccination status Immunity waning Asymptomatic spread Multiple strains

    This is an educational tool, not a forecasting system. It captures the essential spatial dynamics of outbreak spread. Parameters approximate known disease biology but are not calibrated epidemiological estimates.
